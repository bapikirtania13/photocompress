Frequently Asked Questions

Is the image processor really free to use?

Yes, Professional Image Processor is completely free to use. There are no hidden fees, registration requirements, or premium features. All functionality is available to everyone.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, your images are never uploaded to our servers. All processing happens entirely within your browser using client-side technology. Your images remain completely private and secure on your device.

What image formats are supported?

You can upload most common image formats (JPEG, PNG, GIF, BMP, WebP) and convert them to JPEG, PNG, or WebP. You can also export processed images as PDF documents.

Why isn't the tool working in my browser?

The tool requires a modern browser with HTML5 Canvas support. We recommend using the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge. Older browsers may not support all features.

Can I process very large images?

The tool can handle reasonably large images, but performance depends on your device's capabilities. Very large images (>50MB) may cause performance issues or browser crashes. For best results, use images under 20MB.

How can I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Please use the contact form above or email us directly. When reporting bugs, include your browser information and steps to reproduce the issue. We appreciate all feedback and feature suggestions!
