Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 21, 2025

Your Privacy is Our Priority

Professional Image Processor operates with complete client-side processing, which means your images never leave your device. We cannot access, view, store, or transmit your images in any way.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We do NOT collect any personal information such as:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Account registration data
  • Payment information
  • Personal identifiers

1.2 Image Data

We do NOT collect, store, or access your images because:

  • All image processing occurs entirely within your browser
  • Images are never uploaded to our servers
  • Your images remain on your device at all times
  • We have no technical capability to access your images

1.3 Usage Analytics (Optional)

We may collect anonymous, non-personal usage statistics such as:

  • Number of visitors to the website
  • General geographic location (country/region level)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Features used (without any image content data)

This data is collected through standard web analytics tools and cannot be used to identify individual users.

2. How We Use Information

2.1 Service Operation

Since our service operates client-side, we use minimal information only to:

  • Serve the web application to your browser
  • Ensure proper functionality across different devices
  • Improve user experience and fix technical issues

2.2 Analytics and Improvement

Anonymous usage data helps us:

  • Understand which features are most popular
  • Identify technical issues and improve performance
  • Make informed decisions about new features
  • Optimize the website for different browsers and devices

3. Data Security

3.1 Client-Side Processing

Our security model is built on the principle of client-side processing:

  • Your images are processed using HTML5 Canvas in your browser
  • No data transmission to external servers occurs
  • All processing happens locally on your device
  • You maintain complete control over your data

3.2 Technical Security

We implement standard web security practices:

  • HTTPS encryption for all web traffic
  • Secure hosting infrastructure
  • Regular security updates and maintenance
  • Protection against common web vulnerabilities

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 JavaScript Libraries

Our service uses the following client-side libraries:

  • jsPDF: For PDF creation (runs in your browser)
  • JSZip: For creating ZIP files (runs in your browser)
  • Font Awesome: For icons (loaded from CDN)

These libraries operate within your browser and do not transmit your data.

4.2 Web Analytics

We may use web analytics services such as:

  • Google Analytics (with anonymized IP addresses)
  • Other privacy-focused analytics tools

These services collect only anonymous, aggregated data and are configured for maximum privacy protection.

4.3 Content Delivery Networks (CDN)

We use CDNs to deliver resources like fonts and icons. These services may log basic request information but do not access your image data.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

5.1 Cookies

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Basic website functionality
  • Remembering user preferences (if any)
  • Analytics (anonymous data only)

5.2 Local Storage

Your browser may temporarily store:

  • User interface preferences
  • Processing settings
  • Cached resources for better performance

No image data is stored in local storage.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Data Control

Since we don't collect personal data or images, you maintain complete control. You can:

  • Use the service without providing any personal information
  • Clear your browser's cache and cookies at any time
  • Use the service with browser privacy modes
  • Block analytics tracking if desired

6.2 Opt-Out Options

You can opt out of analytics tracking by:

  • Using browser ad blockers
  • Enabling "Do Not Track" in your browser
  • Using privacy-focused browsers
  • Disabling JavaScript (though this will prevent the service from working)

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is suitable for all ages as we:

  • Do not collect any personal information
  • Do not require registration or accounts
  • Do not store any user data
  • Operate with complete privacy by design

8. International Users

Our privacy practices comply with major privacy regulations including:

  • GDPR (EU): Full compliance through privacy-by-design approach
  • CCPA (California): No personal data collection eliminates most obligations
  • Other regional laws: Our minimal data approach ensures broad compliance

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to:

  • Reflect changes in our practices
  • Address new privacy regulations
  • Improve clarity and transparency

Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Data Breach Protocol

In the unlikely event of a security incident:

  • Your images cannot be compromised as they never leave your device
  • Any potential breach would involve only anonymous usage data
  • We would promptly investigate and address any security issues
  • Users would be notified if any significant changes are needed

We are committed to transparency:

  • We have never received requests for user data (as we don't collect any)
  • We have never been required to implement backdoors or tracking
  • Our client-side architecture makes surveillance technically impossible
  • We will update this section if our transparency status changes

Privacy Summary

Bottom Line: Professional Image Processor is designed with privacy as the core principle. Your images are processed entirely within your browser, we collect no personal information, and your privacy is protected by our technical architecture rather than just policies.
